Structure of the program: MULTIPLE ENTRY and MULTIPLE EXIT


There are three levels in the Online M. Tech in AI & DS and to get the M. Tech Degree in Artificial Intelligence & Data Science from NIT Tiruchirappalli, a learner must successfully complete all three levels.

This Course allows to Enter for Two-year M.Tech degree program and facilitates to pre-exit in Two ways with certification/PG Diploma, if needed. Depending on the courses completed and credits earned, the learner can receive a Certification or Advanced PG Diploma or M. Tech Degree in Artificial Intelligence & Data Science from NIT Tiruchirappalli.


Certification in Artificial Intelligence and Data Science (AI and DS)


  • Minimum credits to be earned: [16 Credits]
  • Duration: Six months
  • Eligibility Criteria: Any one of BE/BTech (approved by Senate) or M.Sc (CS/IT/SE) or MCA
  • On completing group 1 courses with eligibility criteria and duration satisfied Certification in AI and DS will be awarded.

Advanced Post-graduate Diploma in AI and DS


  • Minimum credits to be earned: [33 Credits]
  • Duration: 12-24 months
  • Eligibility Criteria: Certification in AI and DS
  • On completing group 2 courses with eligibility criteria and duration satisfied Advanced Post Graduate Diploma in AI and DS will be awarded.

Classwork Timings:


The learner can choose either of the below:

  • Weekdays (6:00 PM to 8:30 PM) and Saturday (8:30 AM to 5:00 PM)
  • Both Saturday & Sunday (8:30 AM to 5:00 PM)

Assessments:


Each course will have tailor-made assessment models viz. group tasks, assignments, report on field visit, quizzes, open book tests, laboratory exercises, mini-project, and end of course summative assessment, etc.

There are three types of assessments in each course:


  1. Cycle Test of 20 marks [Total 2 CTs for each course] (20 x 2 = 40)
  2. Assignment/Quiz/Report for 10 marks
  3. Final Assessment Test for 50 marks

Mode of Exam:


Both the Cycle Tests and Assignment/Quiz will be conducted online as per the schedule of the course.

Final Assessment Test will be conducted Offline at NIT Tiruchirappalli. The learners will write the final exams at the institute, and accommodation will be provided for learners at institute hostels and guest house (based on availability).

Upon requests, Final assessment tests will be conducted at some designated centres decided by the organizing committee.
