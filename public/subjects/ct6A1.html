CT6A1 - HUMAN RESOURCE ANALYTICS

Course Objective(s):

  1. To provide knowledge on various frameworks and hands-on analytical approaches for identifying the business contributions of the HR function in the organizations.

Overview of HR Process - HR Analytics – An overview - Role of analytics in HR - HRM in changing context - Transition from transaction orientation to analytics orientation - HR Analytics Framework – People Capability Maturity Model – LAMP framework – HCM 21 framework – Talent analytics maturity model.

Alignment Analytics – link HR process and organization process - identify alignment opportunities; Human Capital Strategy - HR Alignment Inventory - workforce planning - Measurement Map – Lead and Lag indicators.

Formulation of key performance indicators and key result areas; HR Metrics – Recruitment metrics – Training and development metrics - Talent retention metrics –HR cost benefit metrics – Career Progression Metrics - Performance metrics – Diversity and Inclusion Metrics - Human capital ROI - Designing and Implementing HR Scorecard -Conducting HR Practice Audits.

Descriptive Analytics – Exploring the people data – slice and dice of data - HR Dashboards - Segmentation - Business Insights - KPI Catalogue Creation.

Predicting future performance - techniques to capture the fallouts of HR Practices - Data driven decision making, organization change and improvement.

Course Outcomes :

Students will be able to

  1. Empathize the knowledge of various frameworks and hands-on analytical approaches for identifying the business contributions of the HR function in the organizations.
