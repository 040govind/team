CT6B6 - SUPPLY CHAIN ANALYTICS

Course Objective(s):

  1. To provide the knowledge and necessary skills for carrying out job roles in the domain of Supply chain analytics.

Demand Planning- Review of Forecasting and planning concepts- Defining KPIs-Forecasting Model Building-Discrete and continuous manufacturing.

Supply planning- Procurement and Strategic Sourcing - Inventory Modelling-aggregate planning and resource allocation decisions- Procurement Analytics- Production modelling.

Demand Fulfilment- DC location and network design-optimizing inventory levels in distribution network- Logistics & Network Modelling- Transportation modelling-delayed differentiation, mass customization.

Advanced and business supply chain related topics like CPFR, DDSN, Make/Buy Case Study- Total Supply Chain Cost- computation of transfer prices -revenue management-yield management -product changes/economies of scale.

Undertaking projects with industry inputs- validation of models- frameworks- Review of data analytics techniques-choice of tools and designing solution approach to specific applications.

Course Outcomes :

Students will be able to

  1. Understanding the knowledge and necessary skills for carrying out job roles in the domain of data analytics.
