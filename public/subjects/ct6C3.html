CT6C3 - Federated Learning

Course Objective(s):

  1. To introduce the concept, technologies, systems and applications related to federated learning (FL).
  2. To acquire fundamental knowledge of data privacy and security, privacy-preserving machine learning and distributed AI.

Course Outcomes :

Students will be able to

  1. Understand of the basic concepts, architecture and applications of FL.
  2. Analyse research and application trends in FL.
  3. Deploy real-world FL projects.
  4. Apply FL tools to solve privacy-preserving AI challenges.
