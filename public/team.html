Faculty Coordinators

Dr. P. J. A. Alphonse

Dr. P.J.A. Alphonse, Professor in the Department of Computer Applications, National Institute of Technology Tiruchirappalli has been with the institute since Year 2000. He completed his master’s degree from IIT Delhi and PhD from National Institute of Technology Tiruchirappalli. His research interests are in the areas of Graph Theory, Data Science, Internet of Things, and Deep Learning.
Email Id: alphonse@nitt.edu

Dr. G. R. Gangadharan

“Dr. G. R. Gangadharan, Associate Professor in the Department of Computer Applications, National Institute of Technology Tiruchirappalli, has been with the institute since May 2018. He has received Ph.D. degree in Information and Communication Technology from the University of Trento, Trento, Italy, and European University Association. His research interests are mainly located on the interface between technology and business perspectives. He is a Senior Member of IEEE and ACM. “
Email Id: ganga@nitt.edu

Dr. Vinay Raj

“Dr. Vinay Raj, Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Applications, National Institute of Technology Tiruchirappalli, has been with the institute since October 2022. He has earned his master’s degree from BITS Pilani and PhD from NIT Warangal. His research interests are in the areas of Software Engineering, Machine Learning, Service Oriented Architecture, Microservices, Serverless Computing.“
Email Id: vinayraj@nitt.edu
